How it works

01

Install once

Run the installer and connect your Google account. Threde only requests read-only access to your Gmail.

02

Wake up to a briefing

Every morning at 5 AM, Threde scans your inbox for newsletters, summarises them, and has the briefing ready in your browser.

03

Read what matters

A single scrollable page per day. Sources listed, key points extracted, nothing you didn't ask for.

04

Stays on your machine

No cloud sync, no account, no subscription. Your emails and summaries never leave your computer.

Before you install

  • macOS 13 Ventura or later (Apple Silicon or Intel)
  • A Google account — Gmail is used to fetch your newsletters
  • A free Gemini API key from Google AI Studio — used to summarise emails on-device
  • No Python or developer tools required — everything is bundled
