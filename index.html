How it works

01

Sign in with Google

Connect your Gmail account. Threde only requests read-only access to your newsletters.

02

Wake up to a briefing

Every morning at 5 AM, Threde reads your newsletters, summarizes them, and builds a briefing on your phone.

03

Read what matters

Stories ranked by importance, key quotes pulled, sources cited. One screen with everything you need to know.

04

Tell it what you care about

Vote on stories and track interests. Threde learns what matters to you and gets sharper every day.

What you need

  • An iPhone running iOS 17 or later
  • A Google account — used to sign in and grant read-only Gmail access
  • Newsletter subscriptions in your Gmail inbox
