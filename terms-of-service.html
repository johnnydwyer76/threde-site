Section 4

Data Processing and Third-Party Services

Threde operates through integrations with third-party services. Your use of Threde involves the following data flows:

  • Gmail API (Read-Only): Threde accesses your Gmail account using Google’s OAuth 2.0 authorization framework with read-only permissions. Threde cannot send, delete, modify, or mark email on your behalf.
  • Apple Intelligence (On-Device): Threde uses Apple’s on-device AI to filter and classify incoming emails. This processing happens entirely on your iPhone and no email content leaves your device during this step.
  • Google Gemini API: Newsletter emails that pass the on-device filter are transmitted to Google’s Gemini AI service for deep reading, summarization, and analysis. This transmission is necessary for Threde to function.
  • Local Storage: Briefing archives, editorial memory, your reader profile, and usage feedback are stored on your local device. They are not transmitted to any server operated by Threde’s owner.

Your use of these third-party services is governed by their respective terms of service and privacy policies. Threde’s owner is not responsible for the data practices, availability, or conduct of Google or any other third party.
